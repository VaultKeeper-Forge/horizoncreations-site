How It Works

Keeping the process simple makes it easier to start a conversation and figure out whether a build makes sense.

Step 1

See the style

Look through Standard Pieces, Custom Pieces, or Workbench to get a feel for materials, finish, and the kind of work coming off the bench.

Step 2

Send the idea

Message on Instagram or Facebook with what you need, what it should do, and anything you want it to fit or match.

Step 3

Build from there

From quick makes to one-off commissions, the next step is figuring out the right format instead of forcing every project into a store listing.
